How to play this game

Main Purpose

Random musical notes will pop up! You will have 5 seconds to click on each one using the piano. The piano will have two sets of note, known as octaves, meaning that each note will appear at least two times!

Basic Music Knowledge

Each octave has all the natural notes which are: A,B,C,D,E,F,G . These notes are the white keys on the piano.

The black keys are the half-tones and there are five of them! Half-tones are half-step above or below a natural note. Two symbols determine these notes, the "#" sharp, that means half-step higher , and the "b" flat, that means half-step lower.

E.g. a "Gb" , G flat note that is the black key before the G note and after the F note. The Gb is the same key as the F#, meaning that in some cases the same key can be two different but same sounding notes. There are 5 half-tone notes.

An octave starts with an E note, and the other notes follow alphabetically meaning the order is like this: "E,F,G,A,B,C,D".
